# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 14, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to TouchGrass ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data in a transparent and responsible way. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the TouchGrass mobile and web application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Username** – a unique identifier you choose
- **Email address** – used to identify your account
- **Profile information** – any optional details you add to your profile

### 2. Authentication Data

If you sign in using a third-party provider, we receive basic profile information from that provider:
- **Sign in with Google** – we receive your name, email address, and profile picture as provided by Google
- **Sign in with Apple** – we receive your name and email address (or relay email) as provided by Apple

We do not receive or store your third-party account passwords.

### 3. Habit and Activity Data

To provide the core functionality of the App, we collect:
- **Habits** – the habit names, descriptions, and target frequencies you create
- **Activity logs** – records of when you mark a habit as completed
- **Photos and images** – pictures you upload to document your activities, which are stored securely on AWS S3
- **Captions and notes** – any text you attach to uploaded photos

### 4. Social Data

To support the social features of the App, we collect:
- **Friend connections** – the list of users you have added as friends
- **Activity feed data** – activity updates that are shared with your friends within the App

### 5. Communications and Notifications

We may collect:
- **Push notification tokens** – to send you reminders and updates
- **Notification preferences** – your settings for receiving notifications

### 6. Technical and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the App, including:
- **Device information** – device type, operating system, and version
- **Log data** – IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps, and crash reports
- **Usage data** – features used, interaction patterns, and session duration

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and operate the App** – create and manage your account, store your habits and photos, and deliver core features
2. **Enable social features** – allow you to find and connect with friends, and share activity updates
3. **Send notifications** – remind you to complete your habits and notify you of friends' activity
4. **Improve the App** – analyse usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features
5. **Ensure security** – detect and prevent fraudulent activity, abuse, or security incidents
6. **Comply with legal obligations** – respond to legal requests and enforce our Terms of Service

---

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### With Other Users

- Your **username** and **profile picture** are visible to other users
- Your **activity updates** (habit completions and uploaded photos) are visible to your friends within the App, unless you configure otherwise in your privacy settings

### With Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate the App. These providers only process your data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality obligations. Our current providers include:

| Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Amazon Web Services (AWS S3) | Photo and file storage | [aws.amazon.com/privacy](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/) |

| Apple | Authentication (Sign in with Apple) | [apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) |

### For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

### Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within **30 days**, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

Uploaded photos are deleted from our storage systems within **30 days** of account deletion.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** – request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** – request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion** – request that we delete your account and associated data

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure cloud storage with access controls
- JWT-based authentication with short-lived tokens
- Regular security reviews

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
